Welcome to the Claraoke Database

Due to the removal of Clara's Youtube channel videos following her graduation, the links on this site at this time will not work for links to her videos as well as individual song links. In the future another implementation may be made to replace this functionality.

This site allows you to search a database of almost every karaoke song that Clara has sung with links to listen to from archived streams. The menu to the left has options to search as well as view a full listing of databased streams and their song list.

Disclaimer: This database is no where near perfect, there have been many different contributors, who each have their own style of formatting their timestamp lists. Check the About page for more information.
